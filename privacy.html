VlogCamera — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

VlogCamera (“the app”, “we”) is a dual-camera recording app. We built it to respect your privacy: the app runs entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data.

1. What the app accesses

  • Camera — used to record your front and back cameras at the same time. Video is processed only on your device.
  • Microphone — used to record audio together with your video.
  • Photo Library — with your permission, the app saves the videos you record to your device’s photo library.

2. What we do NOT collect

  • No accounts, names, emails, or contact information.
  • No analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.
  • No uploading of your videos, audio, or photos. Your recordings never leave your device through the app.
  • We cannot see your recordings or photo library beyond writing the videos you choose to record.

3. Data stored on your device

The app stores your preferences (such as language, theme, resolution, frame rate, and picture-in-picture settings) locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is removed when you delete the app.

4. Permissions

You can grant or revoke camera, microphone, and photo access at any time in the iOS Settings app. Revoking camera or microphone access will disable recording.

5. Children’s privacy

The app collects no personal data from anyone, including children.

6. Third parties

The app contains no third-party analytics or advertising SDKs and shares no data with third parties.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will appear here with a new “Last updated” date.
